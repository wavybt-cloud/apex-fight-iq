How It Works

Is the model independent of Vegas?+
Yes. Apex prices every fight from fighter stats, style matchups and form — not from the betting market. Paste a book line to see where the model disagrees (the edge).
What do the numbers mean?+
Each fight shows the AI moneyline for both fighters and an over/under on total rounds. "Market edge" is the gap between the model's implied probability and the price you paste.
What's free vs Pro?+
Prelim lines, model win %, the track record and the free-play tournament are open to everyone. Pro unlocks main-card picks, edge %, method-of-victory, Kelly sizing and the full AI Analyzer.
Is the tournament real money?+
No — the tournament is free-play with virtual coins for leaderboard bragging rights only. It is not a sportsbook and there are no cash buy-ins or payouts.
Is this betting advice?+
No. Everything here is model output for entertainment and research. 21+. Please wager responsibly — 1-800-GAMBLER (US) · begambleaware.org (UK).
